UNCITRAL

The rules of world commerce.

President’s Letter

Dear Delegates,

The United Nations Commission on International Trade Law is the core legal body of the UN system in the field of international trade. Delegates work to harmonise and modernise the law of cross-border commerce through conventions, model laws and common standards.

We look forward to welcoming you this September.

— The Chairing Panel
