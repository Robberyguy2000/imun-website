What is IMUN?

Model United Nations is a simulation of the UN and other international bodies: students take on the roles of delegates, judges and ministers, research their assigned positions, and debate the questions shaping the world — from refugee protection to nuclear safeguards.

IMUN is the International Academy — Amman’s annual conference, welcoming delegates from schools across Jordan and beyond. Over three days, seventeen committees run in two languages, spanning classic UN organs, regional councils, courts and full-crisis cabinets.
